Today's Kenyan Specials

Check Out Our Kenyan Dishes

Nyama Choma

Kenya's national dish of barbecued meat, usually goat or beef, cooked over an open flame.

This dish is perfect for social gatherings, served with kachumbari (tomato salad) and Ugali. A classic favorite in Kenyan cuisine.

Ugali

A staple food made from maize or other grains boiled into a thick dough-like consistency.

Ugali is often served as the base for various Kenyan dishes, accompanied by vegetables, stew, or meat. Simple, yet hearty!

Matoke

A delicious stew made from green bananas, cooked with tomatoes, onions, garlic, and spices.

Matoke is often served with Ugali, rice, or chapati and pairs well with roasted meat or fried fish. It’s a popular home-cooked dish.

Kenyan Pilau

A fragrant rice dish cooked with a blend of aromatic spices, served with a side of fresh tomato and onion salad.

Kenyan Pilau is a festive dish, often served at special events and celebrations. It’s flavorful, filling, and perfect for a family meal.

Kuku Paka

A delicious chicken dish cooked in a rich and creamy coconut curry sauce.

Kuku Paka is a perfect combination of tender chicken and the richness of coconut milk, served with rice or Ugali. A coastal delicacy loved by many!

Ingoho

Fried chicken marinated with spices, garlic, ginger, and sometimes chili for a flavorful kick.

Ingoho is the perfect meal for chicken lovers, served with a side of Ugali or fried potatoes. A crispy, juicy treat!
